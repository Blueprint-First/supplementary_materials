[INJECTED CONSTRAINTS — extracted verbatim from the SCA TravelPlanner blueprint]

You MUST satisfy every rule below when producing the final travel plan.
Each rule corresponds to a constraint enforced by the official TravelPlanner
scorer (commonsense_constraint.py + hard_constraint.py).

— Commonsense constraints (always enforced) —
C1. Reasonable visiting city. Day 1 MUST depart from `org`. The trip MUST be
    a closed loop ending in `org`. Each intermediate city MUST appear on
    consecutive days (a city cannot be re-entered after leaving). For trips
    longer than 3 days, every visited city MUST lie inside the destination
    state. Every city name MUST exist in citySet_with_states.txt.
C2. No restaurant repetition. Across the entire itinerary the same
    restaurant MUST NOT appear more than once.
C3. No attraction repetition. Across the entire itinerary the same
    attraction MUST NOT appear more than once.
C4. Transportation consistency. Day 1 MUST have a non-empty transportation
    field. `Self-driving` MUST NOT co-occur with `Flight`, and
    `Self-driving` MUST NOT co-occur with `Taxi`.
C5. Information localized to the current city. Restaurants and attractions
    belong to the day's current city; on a travel day accommodation belongs
    to the destination city.
C6. Database existence. Every flight number, route, restaurant, attraction
    and accommodation MUST be retrievable from the TravelPlanner sandbox.
C7. Accommodation minimum-nights rule. Consecutive nights at the same
    accommodation MUST satisfy its `minimum nights`.
C8. Information completeness. Required fields populated each day; non-`-`
    field ratio MUST be ≥ 50%.

— Hard constraints (enforced when present in the query) —
H1. Budget cap. Total cost ≤ user budget. Cost = transportation + meals +
    lodging, scaled by people count and per-vehicle / per-room occupancy.
H2. Cuisine coverage. All required cuisines tasted at the destination.
H3. Room rule. Chosen accommodation does not prohibit any required rule
    among {smoking, parties, children under 10, visitors, pets}.
H4. Room type. Chosen accommodation matches required type.
H5. Transportation restriction. Honor `no flight` / `no self-driving` if
    specified.

These rules are MANDATORY. Any violation causes the plan to fail.
